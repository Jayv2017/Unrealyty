U N R E A L Y T Y

(uhn路ree路a路luh路tee)

noun

Origin: Derived from the term 'Reality'
Reality is the state of things as they actually exist, rather than as they may appear or might be imagined.

Unrealyty is a project with the aim of encouraging people to explore the intersection of science and imagination. We hope to bring down the bariers of reality by linking random "midnight thoughts" with theories of science.
